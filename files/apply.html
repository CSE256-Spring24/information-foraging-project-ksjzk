Application Procedure

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

step 1

Fill out the application form. You may also print out and return the pdf version: Application for Admission WUNS. An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school.

step 2

After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date.

step 3

Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

step 4

You are all set. Enjoy!
